How it works

From sign-up to showtime in four steps

01

Connect your account

Sign in and link your TikTok LIVE creator handle to build your LiveMatch profile.

02

Get matched

Browse opponents in your league and send a challenge — or hit Find a Battle Now for an instant matchup.

03

Track & climb

Your stats update after every battle. Win matchups, earn fragments, and rise through the leagues.
