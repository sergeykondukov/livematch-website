Frequently asked questions

What is LiveMatch?

LiveMatch is a matchmaking app for TikTok LIVE creators. It helps you find ranked battle opponents at your level, schedule battles, track your performance stats, and climb through the leagues.

How do I find a battle?

Open the Match tab, filter by Same Rank, ±1 Rank, or Any, and send a challenge to a creator. You can also tap Find a Battle Now on the Home screen for a quick matchup. Once an opponent accepts, confirm a time on the Schedule tab.

How do leagues and rankings work?

You earn fragments and climb leagues based on your battles and outcomes. Your rank, diamonds, and stats update after each battle, and you can see where you stand on your league's leaderboard.

How do I connect my TikTok account?

During setup you'll be asked to link your TikTok creator handle. You can disconnect it any time from your LiveMatch settings, which stops LiveMatch from receiving new data from TikTok.

How do I delete my account or my data?

You can delete your account from the app's settings, which removes your profile from LiveMatch. To request deletion or export of your data, email privacy@livematchnetwork.com. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is LiveMatch affiliated with TikTok?

No. LiveMatch is an independent app built for TikTok LIVE creators. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok or ByteDance Ltd. Your battles happen on TikTok and remain subject to TikTok's own rules and policies.
