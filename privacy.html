2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. Your display name, username, profile photo or avatar, and the TikTok creator handle you link to LiveMatch.
  • Profile and preferences. Your league, matchmaking preferences (for example Same Rank, ±1 Rank, or Any), and availability for scheduling.
  • Communications. Messages you send to other creators through the Service, and any messages you send to our support team.

Information created through your use of the Service

  • Battle and performance data. Records of battles you schedule and complete, including show-up rate, battle minutes, number of battles, skips, average duration, diamonds, fragments, league rank, and win/loss outcomes.
  • Scheduling activity. Battle invitations, acceptances, cancellations, and timing.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical data. Device model, operating system, app version, language, and similar technical identifiers.
  • Usage data. How you interact with the Service — features used, screens viewed, and general activity logs.
  • Notifications. If you enable them, we use push notification tokens to send match alerts and reminders.
